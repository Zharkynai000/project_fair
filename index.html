The Icon returns. BRABUS 800 SUPERBLACK.

The BRABUS 800 SUPERBLACK is the return of a legend and the beginning of a new era. With 800 unstoppable horsepower under its powerdome and a striking, all-black flair, this next-level luxury Geländewagen once again defines the true meaning behind the word “iconic”. It doesn’t just follow in the footsteps of its predecessors—it blazes its own trail. Based on the W 465 series Mercedes-AMG G 63, it is defined by a completely reimagined carbon exterior concept as well as a new fusion of power, elegance and 100% monochrome-cool luxury.

Design & Exterior. Back in Black.

Beyond infusing the BRABUS Collection 2025 with a unique touch of Superblack, the BRABUS 800 takes our Monochrome Design Series to new heights. It is a thrilling fusion of minimalism and sheer, unstoppable power. Its signature black-on-black aesthetic is not just a design choice; it is an attitude, created specifically for those who refuse to settle for anything less than the maximum. The supercar’s exclusive WIDESTAR exterior concept is made entirely of carbon, sealed and polished to a high gloss to deliver an incomparable 1-Second-Wow effect.

In addition to the WIDESTAR front apron with its large air intakes and integrated front spoiler, a range of BRABUS typical design highlights, including the BRABUS Powerdome attachment for the hood, the WIDESTAR fenders and the roof element with LED high beams give the all-wheel-drive icon its extremely dynamic, BRABUS-typical face.

The WIDESTAR fenders, featuring integrated LED entry lights for the running boards and visible carbon mirror caps, command attention in the side view. The unmistakable tail end of this supercar is likewise characterized by a variety of new WIDESTAR carbon elements, true to our Black and Bold design philosophy. These include the rear fenders, the rear apron and the spare wheel cover with embossed BRABUS lettering. The new BRABUS rear wing completes the look with aerodynamic precision, adding a striking flair perfectly tailored to the G-Class’s bold physique.

Power & Sound. Unleashed.

The number "800" on the sides and rear of this supercar is an instant giveaway for any enthusiast. Courtesy of the BRABUS engineering team’s latest innovations, the SUPERBLACK features a four-liter, eight-cylinder biturbo engine with 588 kW / 800 hp at 6,400 rpm and a maximum torque of 1,000 Nm, which is constantly available between 3,800 and 4,400 rpm.

The BRABUS 800 high-performance engine with two specially developed BRABUS turbochargers catapults the Ultra Luxury SUV from 0 to 100 km/h in just 4.0 seconds thanks to the perfect interplay between the powerplant, SPEEDSHIFT TCT nine-speed transmission and the sophisticated all-wheel drive system. You’re in control. Shift the car manually or let the automatic system do the work with the ergonomically shaped BRABUS carbon paddle shifters on the steering wheel. The supercar’s top speed is electronically limited to 240 km/h.

The BRABUS stainless steel sports exhaust system with active valve control is the optimal choice for performance optimization and offers a soundscape that adapts to any situation. At the touch of a button, you can switch between a throaty V8 sound in sport mode and a discreet whisper in "Coming Home" mode. The BRABUS exhaust system is a visual showstopper, too. It features red lighting integrated into the visible carbon trims for the two sidepipe-style tailpipes positioned in front of the rear wheels on either side of the vehicle.

WHEELS & SUSPENSION.Iconic.

Another unmistakable highlight of the BRABUS 800 SUPERBLACK‘s unique signature character are the BRABUS Monoblock ZV “PLATINUM EDITION” high-end wheels with a kingsize diameter of 24 inches. Their 20-spoke construction in all-black perfectly matches the “Superblack” motto and impresses with a particularly sporty look underlined by the BRABUS typical center lock design. They are fitted with specially developed Hankook Ventus S1 evo ZX high-performance tires in the sizes 295/30 ZR 24 at the front axle and 355/25 ZR 24 at the rear axle.
